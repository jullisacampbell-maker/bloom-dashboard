FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers for Bloom OS users.

What is Bloom OS?

Bloom OS is a calm family command center for planning your day, week, meals, homeschool rhythm, goals, and family life.

Is Bloom OS free?

Bloom Starter is free. Pro and Family plans unlock additional planning, memory, homeschool, and family features.

Does my data save?

Yes. When you are signed in, Bloom can save your information to your Bloom account so it can sync across devices.

Why do I need an account?

An account lets Bloom save your profile, plans, and progress securely so you can return later or use another device.

How do I reset my password?

Use the password reset option on the login screen. If you still need help, contact support.

How do I upgrade?

Choose Pro or Family from the upgrade screen. Paid plan access is connected to your account email.

Can I cancel?

Yes. You can cancel a subscription anytime. Access continues through the end of your billing period.
