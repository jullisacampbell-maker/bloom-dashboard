Bloom OS Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Overview

Bloom OS is created by Jullisa Blooms to help users organize family life, planning, meals, goals, and routines.

Information we collect

We may collect information you provide, including your email address, account details, profile information, planning notes, meal notes, family organization details, and support messages.

How we use information

We use your information to provide Bloom OS, save your account data, sync your information across devices, improve the product, provide support, and manage subscriptions.

Cloud sync

If you create an account, your Bloom data may be stored in a cloud database connected to your account so you can access it from multiple devices.

Payments

Payments may be processed by third-party payment providers such as Stripe. Bloom OS does not store your full payment card details.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may use trusted service providers to operate the app, process payments, or provide support.

Your choices

You may contact support to request help with your account or data.

Contact

Questions? Email support@jullisablooms.com.

This simple policy is a starting point and should be reviewed by a legal professional before larger public launch.
